A simple man. A simple mission.

Established in 2014, Dave's Royal Artisinal Jelly was created with the simple purpose of creating quality, hand-crafted jellies to be enjoyed by the world at large. Handcrafted and free from the additives and toxins found in preserves mass produced by 'big jelly'.
